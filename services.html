What WebNest builds
WebNest focuses on small, focused sites that help people actually find rooms, rentals, or services without getting lost in menus or slow pages.

Core service: hotel & stay websites

  • Landing pages for hotels, motels, and short stays.
  • Room cards that show price ranges, features, and location.
  • Filters and search so guests can find what fits them.
  • Links out to booking systems or contact info.

Housing & rentals pages

  • Listings for apartments, studios, and houses.
  • Tags like “pool”, “pet friendly”, “long stay”, “student”.
  • Address, price ranges, and simple descriptions.
  • Can be used by one agent, landlord, or a small team.

Small business sites

  • One-page websites for local services.
  • Sections for what you do, prices, and how to reach you.
  • Clean design that looks good on phones.
