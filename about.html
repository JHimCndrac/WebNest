About
What WebNest is and who’s behind it
WebNest is a small personal project built in Phoenix by a 16-year-old student who is learning software engineering by working on real websites instead of only school assignments.

WebNest in simple words

WebNest focuses on clean, simple sites for hotels, short-term stays, and real estate. The goal is not to build huge, complicated apps — it’s to create pages that are clear, fast, and easy for normal people to use on their phone or laptop.

Why hotels and housing?

  • People always need places to stay and places to live.
  • Even one small hotel or landlord can benefit from a good website.
  • Listings and booking pages are a real skill in web dev and software engineering.
Built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — the same core tools used in bigger software projects, just kept small and focused for now.

About the developer

WebNest is built by a 16-year-old in Phoenix who is already in college and aiming for software engineering. The idea is to learn by actually shipping projects, not just watching tutorials.

Focus areas

Values

Clear information Fast loading Mobile-friendly Easy to share

If you ever wanted a simple site for your rooms, rentals, or small business, WebNest is built to be a starting point that can grow with real needs.
